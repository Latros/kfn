Kingsclear First Nation offers a wide range of activities. We sponsor organized hockey and Bonnie Kilburn Dance Club. We have a gym and offer several activities, including:

  • Floor hockey
  • Soccer-baseball
  • Volleyball
  • Basketball
  • Soccer
  • Dodgeball
  • Softball
  • Archery
  • T-ball
  • Swimming
  • Washer toss
  • Darts
  • Canoeing
  • Cross-country biking
  • Track & field

We also organize Halloween parties, summer camping, March Break activities, Summer Games, and Junior Olympics.
