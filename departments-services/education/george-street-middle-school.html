George Street Middle School has a vibrant First Nations classroom established for our First Nations students to enhance their learning opportunities with their Literacy and Numeracy skills being supported by Mrs. Young-Jones. Students also have access to cultural supports such as Mr. Dick Paul for cultural and spiritual guidance and Mr. Walter Paul for Maliseet Language.
